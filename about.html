About QuizGame

QUIZGAME is a mobile brainteaser app for educative and fun purposes. It was created by Stephanie Onyiwa under the ALX Software engineering programme in session 2023/2024. It was inspired by her love for quizeez, puzzles and current affairs. This game is packed with over 20 brainteasers and is suitable for teens and adults
