Welcome to the Quiz Game Tutorial
Are you ready to test your knowledge and challenge your brain?
Our quiz game is designed to entertain and educate, offering a variety of questions that cover a wide range of topics. Before you dive into the excitement, let's go over the basics:
Objective: The goal is simple – answer as many questions correctly as you can to earn points and climb the leaderboard.

How to Play: Click on the "PLAY" button to start the quiz.
Read each question carefully and choose the correct answer from the provided options.
Be mindful of the timer! Some questions may have time limits, so think quickly. After answering all the questions, you'll receive a score based on your performance.

Features:
Multiple Choice: Most questions are multiple-choice, so select the option you believe is correct.
Score Tracking: Keep track of your scores and try to beat your personal best.
Categories: Explore different categories of questions – from general knowledge to specific themes.
Leaderboard: Compete with other players and see where you stand on the global leaderboard.

Tips for Success:
Stay focused and read each question thoroughly. Don't be afraid to take educated guesses if you're unsure. Use the timer strategically – some questions might require a quick response. Ready to Begin? Click the button and embark on your quiz adventure! Have fun, and may your brainpower shine!
